The Duck River

created by Nicky Lin


This is a little simulation that lets you play with ducks!

Here are the selection of buttons available:

There is also a monitor that keeps track of how many ducks are on the screen:


Controls Description
Add a duck

Creates a little duck that floats across the screen.

Clear the ducks

Tell the ducks on the screen to go home.
You can also click on the body of a duck to make them go away,
but only when they are not paused.

Pause the ducks

When checked, the ducks on the screen stop and take a nap.

Lots of ducks

When checked, the ducks start a marathon.

Path

Choose a path for the ducks to follow.
Choosing "sine" makes the ducks follow a nice sine curve.
Choosing "tangent" makes the ducks freak out and follow a tangent curve.
Choosing "loop" makes the ducks go in a loop-de-loop.

Amplitude

Changes how vertically high the ducks swim.
The greater the amplitude, the higher they go.

Period

Changes how fast the ducks swim.
The shorter the period, the faster the ducks go.
The greater the period, the slower the ducks go.

Amount of ducks

Reports the number of ducks swimming.
